When we’ve had a late night as rockstars tend to do, a morning full of pancakes is always in order. But sometimes, we need those pancakes to really pack a healthy, hearty punch. And when we have those times, we make this protein pancake recipe. Best Protein Pancake Recipe

There’s no sugar here so if you’re trying to focus on your health yet want pancakes, now you can have the best of both worlds and that totally rocks. You’ve got nothing to lose and this is the best protein pancake recipe that will give you a reason to get up in the morning!

Easy Protein Pancake Recipe

This easy and healthy pancake recipe is ready in just 20 minutes from start to finish. You can make a decadent breakfast fit for a rock god in no time. These protein powder pancakes taste amazing, like regular pancakes but fill you up with protein.

Protein Pancake Ingredients

Rolled oats – Unlike other pancake recipes we’ve unleashed, rolled oats are the base for the protein pancake batter. Baking powder – This will help your protein powder pancakes cook up properly. Vanilla whey protein powder – Making protein pancakes with protein powder is key. The vanilla flavor is what will make these pancakes taste amazing. Cinnamon – adds more flavor to your protein pancake mix. Salt – Builds that flavor contrast. Eggs – Binds all the ingredients together to make these protein pancakes with oats. Greek yogurt – Use plain to add that moisture to your batter. Coconut oil – Your necessary fat for pancakes that won’t be dry. Vanilla extract – For more warmth in the flavors.

How do you make protein pancakes from scratch?

Put your rolled oats into a blender on high until it turns into powder. Then add the rest of the ingredients and blend well. Lightly grease your nonstick pan over medium heat. Then scoop in your batter, cooking for 1 to 2 minutes until the bubbles form on the top. Flip and cook another 1 to 2 minutes, then serve right away with all your favorite toppings.
